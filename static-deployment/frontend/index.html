Blockchain Integration

Our system leverages Ethereum blockchain technology for secure, transparent legal case management:

User Authentication

Secure login with MetaMask wallet ensures your identity is cryptographically verified on the blockchain.

Role-Based Access

Client, lawyer, and judge roles are stored directly on the Ethereum blockchain, ensuring immutable verification.

Case Management

All case details are securely stored on Ethereum, providing a tamper-proof record of all legal proceedings.

Document Verification

Document references are stored on-chain with IPFS integration, ensuring document integrity and authenticity.

How to Login with MetaMask

Benefits of Blockchain Authentication

  • Secure, cryptographic authentication
  • No password to remember or risk being stolen
  • Digital signatures for document verification
  • Immutable audit trail of all actions
